Frequently Asked Questions

What is draw without replacement? ▼

Without replacement means each drawn item is removed from the pool and won't appear again, ensuring every item is picked at most once. Ideal for fair raffles or attendance calls.

How do I set different probabilities? ▼

Enable "Custom Weights" and enter a number for each item. Higher numbers mean higher chances. For example, if A = 2 and B = 1, A has roughly a 2/3 chance of being picked.

Can I generate a numbered set automatically? ▼

Yes. Switch to the "Numbered Set" tab, set your start and end numbers and an optional prefix, then click Generate. Great for seat numbers, contestant IDs, and more.
