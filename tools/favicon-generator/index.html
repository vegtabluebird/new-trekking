Frequently Asked Questions

    

      

        
          What is a favicon?
          ▼
        
        
A favicon (favorite icon) is the small icon that appears in browser tabs, bookmarks, and search results next to your website's name. It helps users identify your site at a glance and is essential for brand recognition.

      

      

        
          What sizes do I need?
          ▼
        
        
Modern websites need: favicon.ico (multi-size, 16+32+48px), favicon-16x16.png, favicon-32x32.png, apple-touch-icon.png (180×180px), and optionally favicon.svg (SVG vector icon for modern browsers). You can download all from this tool.

      

      

        
          Can I use SVG as a favicon?
          ▼
        
        
Yes! Chrome, Firefox, and Edge all support SVG favicons. They look crisp at any size and are tiny in file size. It's best to still provide a favicon.ico as fallback for older browsers that don't support SVG favicons.

      

      

        
          Is my image uploaded to a server?
          ▼
        
        
No. All processing happens entirely in your browser using the Canvas API and JavaScript. Your image never leaves your computer. You can even use this tool offline once the page is loaded.
